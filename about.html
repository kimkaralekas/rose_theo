Welcome to my shop! All cards are made with love from my cozy Austin, TX design studio. Questions? Special orders? Contact me! My goal is to create beautiful unique items that will brighten your day! I work hard to make sure you are completely satisfied with the items from my shop.

What types of designs do you offer?

I create designs for all occasions such as weddings, engagements, birthdays, holidays. I create cards, invitations, thank yous, RSVPs, etc.

How can I request a custom order?

Please contact me and we can go from there. Each custom project is unique, therefore the design time and cost are unique to each custom order.

Do you accept international orders?

Yes! If you live outside of the US, please don't hesitate to contact me. I can determine the additional shipping cost to ship to your location and provide an estimate.

How long will it take to receive my order?

All items are created and shipped within 1 - 3 business days. Custom orders take just a little bit longer, depending on complexity.

Do you accept refunds and/or exchanges?

All sales are final. I do my best to make sure your purchase reaches you safely, but I cannot be responsible for the package once it leaves my hands.
With that said, I want you to be happy with your order, so if you have any questions or concerns, please contact me and we'll figure something out! (:

Are you cards printed in the US or are they outsourced?

All cards are printed in Austin, Texas on recycled paper! I also make sure to include envelopes made from recycled paper.
