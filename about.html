What types of designs do you offer?

I create designs for all occasions such as weddings, engagements, birthdays, holidays. I create cards, invitations, thank yous, RSVPs, etc.

How can I request a custom order?

Please contact me and we can go from there. Each custom project is unique, therefore the design time and cost are unique to each custom order.

Do you accept international orders?

Yes! If you live outside of the US, please don't hesitate to contact me. I can determine the additional shipping cost to ship to your location and provide an estimate.
